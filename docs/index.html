💡 What does this mean?

There's enough clean energy capacity waiting in interconnection queues to power the United States seven times over. These projects — solar farms, wind turbines, battery storage — are built and ready. They're just waiting for permission to connect to the grid. Some will wait 5+ years. Most will never make it.

📍 The IRA Effect

The Inflation Reduction Act passed in August 2022. Queue entries exploded. 2022 saw 4x more projects enter queues than 2018. But grid infrastructure hasn't kept pace — creating massive backlogs.
